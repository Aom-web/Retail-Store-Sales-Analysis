# Retail-Store-Sales-Analysis-EXCEL

## OVERVIEW

 This is an analysis of a retail store sales that contains various columns such as gender, season, item purchased and many more. It helps to know the season that had the highest and lowest purchase and how to restructure the continuation of selling such products at that season, the category of item a gender is likely to purchase much more over the other irrespective of gender bias, the gender who prefers to use discount the most and likely to continue purchasing as deduced from the previous purchase column, and what product has the highest repurchase.
 
## DATA SOURCE

This data was gotten from Kaggle
[Download here] (https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/hassanjameelahmed/store-sales)
